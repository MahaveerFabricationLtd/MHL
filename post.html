Laboratory Management System

This was a project I started back in november 2017 when I first learned Fabrication. I was the sole creator/developer for the first year of the project before I was able to hire help.

Customers had the ability to view all their work with a dynamic search form, trend designs, add good reports and even map their samples' physical location.

I worked on this system for 2 years before.

  • - Gate Components with Designing
  • - Structure Frame
  • - Steel & Iron Work
  • - U Bracket
  • - Column, Trusses
  • - Ceiling Fall
